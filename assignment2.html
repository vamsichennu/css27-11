chicken dum biryani

ingredients:

  • 4 table spoons curd
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoo garam and biryani masala
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 1 lemon

Take a bowl and add chicken and all ingridients into it and marinate ait well and put it aside for 2 hours

meanwhile take another bowl and add two cups of rice and water into it rinse it for two times and soak them for 30 minutes.

now take that rice bowl and put it on the stove and boil it.turn off the stove once rice is half cooked.

now take the marinated chicken bowl and add this half cooked rice into it like layers.

and put it on flame for 20 min our delicious biryani is ready to serve.

preparation time cooking time total time
2 hours 20 mins 2hr20 mins
